About Us

Welcome to the Course Prediction App. We use advanced machine learning models to help high school graduates choose the best university or college courses based on their final grades. Our goal is to provide personalized recommendations to assist students in making informed decisions about their academic future.

Our team is dedicated to leveraging data and technology to support students in their educational journey. If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to reach out to us through the Contact page.
